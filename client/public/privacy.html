Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Personal Life OS ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you authenticate with Google, we request access to specific data to provide our AI assistant features:

  • Basic Profile Information: Your name and email address.
  • Gmail Data (Read-Only): We request read-only access to search and retrieve your emails so the AI assistant can summarize or query your inbox on your behalf.
  • Google Drive Data (Read-Only): We request read-only access to search and retrieve text from your documents to answer your questions based on your own files.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to provide the core functionality of the Personal Life OS application:

  • To securely authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To pass relevant email and document snippets to our AI Language Model (LLM) only when you explicitly ask a question requiring that context.

We do not use your Google data to train AI models. Your data is processed dynamically to answer your queries and is not persistently stored in our databases beyond secure access tokens.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information or Google data to outside parties, except for the secure third-party AI provider (Groq) used exclusively to process your chat requests in real-time. The AI provider does not retain your data.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your Google OAuth tokens are securely stored in our database and are only used during your active sessions.

5. Limited Use Policy

Personal Life OS's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact the developer of this application.
